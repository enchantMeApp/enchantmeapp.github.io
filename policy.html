Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22.12.2025

Enchant Me! respects your privacy. This application is designed to function without collecting, storing, or processing personal user data. The app does not require registration, accounts, or login of any kind.

1. Data Collection

Enchant Me! does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location data, contacts, messages, or any other personal identifiers. All functionality is available without providing personal data.

2. App Functionality and Internet Usage

Enchant Me! requires an active internet connection to operate. The app retrieves prewritten, non-personal content from publicly available text files hosted on GitHub. This includes tarot reading templates, horoscope content, and moon phase information.

This content is downloaded when the app starts and stored temporarily in the app’s memory for use during the current session. No personal information is transmitted during this process, and no data is uploaded from the user’s device.

3. Advertising

Enchant Me! uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect limited device information such as advertising identifiers, device type, and basic app interaction data. This information is used by Google to deliver personalized or non-personalized advertisements, depending on the user’s device and system settings.

Google’s use of data is governed by its own privacy policy, which can be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. User-Stored Data

Enchant Me! allows users to save tarot readings, sigils, spell notes, and related app data locally on their device. The app also allows users to export this data to a local file and import it on another device for migration purposes.

All saved data remains entirely on the user’s device. This data is never uploaded to external servers and is not accessible to the developer. The app accesses this data only to display it locally to the user.

5. Third-Party Services

Enchant Me! uses third-party services strictly for functionality and monetization purposes. These services include Google AdMob for advertising and GitHub for hosting static content files. No personal user data is shared with GitHub. Content is retrieved as static text files only.

6. Children’s Privacy

Enchant Me! is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data, please contact the developer so appropriate action can be taken.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately upon publication.
